The following are instructions for using this template; delete these instructions after use.

  1. Before the end of lab on Friday 23 September, fill in the preliminary information that says when each team member is available for interviews during weeks 2 and 3. Use a simple day/time notation for availability, e.g., MWF 12-3, TR 10-1, F 3-4:30

  2. During week 2, as the interviews are scheduled, fill in the 4-column table with each row containing the information for one client interview.

  3. The Date column has the date and time on which the interview is scheduled to take place.

  4. The Team Members column has the name of the two 308/402 team members who will attend the interview. Typically, there will be one 308 member and one 402 member, but there may be two 308 members in a few cases.

  5. The Client has the name(s) of the client(s) who will be interviewed.

  6. The Location column has the campus building and room number where the interview is scheduled to take place.

  7. When the interviews are fully scheduled, remove the preliminary information, leaving the 4-column scheduling table.
