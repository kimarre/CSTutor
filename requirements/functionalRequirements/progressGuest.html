2.8.1 Progress & Assessment - Guest Interface

This scenario depicts a guest's view of the Progress feature. When users are not signed in, they are given limited "guest" access, which disables many of the features. The following image, Figure 2.8.1.1, shows the message that is displayed to users with Guest access which provides a link to the login screen. Once users log in and click the Progress button in the menu bar, they are taken to either the Instructor or Student Progress screens, depending on their level of access.
