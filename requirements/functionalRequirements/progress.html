2.8. Progress & Assessment

The Progress feature of the CSTutor tool allows students and professors to track their performance in the tutorials. The scenarios in this section show the Progress feature as navigated by a guest user, a student user, and an instructor user. Scenarios include:

  • Viewing a user's progress as a Guest
  • Viewing a user's progress as a Student
  • Viewing a user's progress as an Instructor
