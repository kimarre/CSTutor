1.2. System Personnel

The end users for this project are the students and professors who will use this tool. Additionally, the customers are the professors and/or organizations who will use it. Our team is the project management, along with Dr. Fisher. The domain experts are professors who are experts in the subjects they teach. The members of our team are the system analysts as well as the system developers.
