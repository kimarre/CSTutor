1.5.4. Khan Academy

Khan Academy is a great one-stop shop for learning anything from math and science to history and economics. It offers a wide variety of subjects in the categories of Math, Science, Economics and Finance, Arts and Humanities, Computing, and Test Prep.

Khan Academy uses a unique teaching method, by combining YouTube lectures and examples with comments and questions located on the same page so users may further their learning experience with other active users. Along the subject track, users encounter skill check questions and interactive examples and demonstrations. If someone gets stuck on one of the questions, the page also offers the relevant video lecture so he/she may review that specific topic.

Good Features:

  • Video lectures are clear and offer great examples
  • Assessment quizzes help to determine if you should repeat and review material, or skip ahead if you're more advanced
  • Wide variety of subjects to learn
  • Community based Q&A section allows you to get feedback relatively quickly
  • "Report a mistake" button for every video topic or exercise ensures reinforcement of accurate information

Bad Features:

  • Some people consider the quality of the videos to not be the highest
  • Material is often presented in a somewhat random order

Missing Features:

  • No available transcripts of video lectures
