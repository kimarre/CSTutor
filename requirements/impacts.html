1.4. Impacts

CS Tutor has a positive impact on students by providing help with difficult concepts at a convenient time and place. It provides metrics based on time spent and quiz grades for professors to see what material students are struggling with. It also improves the amount and quality of tutoring offered. One negative impact would be less students attending the Tutoring Center in person. Also, the existence of tutorials from the professors may make students more likely to skip classes.
